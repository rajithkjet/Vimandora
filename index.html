Welcome to Vimandora

Vimandora Design and Construction is a House Planning Designing and Construction company in Sri Lanka with an excellent reputation. We are experts in Planning, Designing and Construction of houses and buildings.

Our strength is our professionally qualified and well-experienced staff who gives their best to exceed customer expectations. The task force is strengthened with professionals from all sectors such as architects, engineers, quantity surveyors, surveyors, mason workers etc. This particular team will handle the project with all kind of supervision and advices until the project is completed according to the plan. We provide different types of services according to client expectations.
